Your Questions Answered

Timeless Traveller is a travel assistant website designed to enrich your travel experience.
It provides historical insights about your current location, recommends top restaurants, hotels,
and attractions, and helps you navigate between locations efficiently.

Signing up for Timeless Traveller is easy! Simply visit our website and click the "Sign Up" button.
You'll be prompted to enter your name, email address, and create a password.
Once you've completed the registration process,
you'll be able to log in and start using our software right away

Currently, Timeless Traveller requires an internet connection to provide accurate location-based services and updated recommendations. However, we are working on adding offline features in the future.

You can report issues or share feedback using the contact form on the website.
Your input is valuable and helps us improve the platform for all users.
